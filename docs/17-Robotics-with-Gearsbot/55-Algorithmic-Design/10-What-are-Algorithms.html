What are Algorithms?

Algorithms are the step-by-step instructions we use to complete a task.

These are all algorithms!

Algorithms can be created in many forms, including...

  • Text (eg. Python, Javascript)
  • Flow charts
  • Blocks (eg. Scratch, GearsBot)
  • Paper and Cardboard!
