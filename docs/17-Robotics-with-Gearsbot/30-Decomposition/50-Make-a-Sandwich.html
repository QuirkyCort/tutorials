Example: Make a Sandwich

Instructor's notes

In this exercise, your teacher is going to play the role of a robot.

Your job is to decompose the steps of making a sandwich, and send the instructions to the teacher robot.

Materials

  • Bread (2 slices)
  • Peanut butter
  • Strawberry jam
  • Popsicle stick

Important!

For the next 5 mins, your teacher is going to be a robot, not a human! Make sure you decompose your instructions into sufficiently small steps for teacher robot.
